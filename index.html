Welcome to our Rayalaseema-inspired restaurant! Immerse yourself in the bold and fiery flavors of this region, known for its spicy and rich culinary traditions.

Begin your journey with tantalizing appetizers like Gongura Chicken, Natukodi Vepudu (country chicken fry), and Kodi Miriyala (chicken pepper) from the heart of Rayalaseema..

--Discover the distinct taste of Jonna Rotte (sorghum bread) and Ragi Sangati, traditional Rayalaseema bread staples..
-- Delight in the robust flavors of Rayalaseema curries, such as the tangy Ulavacharu (horsegram soup) and the spicy Gutti Vankaya (stuffed brinjal).
-- Experience the rustic charm of Rayalaseema's signature dish, Royyala Iguru (prawn curry), made with a delectable blend of spices.
-- Indulge in the fiery Andhra-style spicy chicken and mutton curries, like Kodi Vepudu and Gongura Mamsam.
-- Come and experience the flavors of Rayalaseema at our restaurant, where each dish is crafted with love and passion to ensure an unforgettable culinary journey.
